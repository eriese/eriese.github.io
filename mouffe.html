Bottom of the Mouffe

Click to watch
A 12-minute Bunraku-style puppet piece conceived by Jeanette Plourde and performed to music and without text. Bottom of the Mouffe is a companion piece to La Cienaga. Four puppets are featured in the piece: two 36-inch puppets and two 25-inch puppets.
