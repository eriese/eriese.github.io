Food for the Gods
The ancestors greet one another before the meal.
Food for the Gods was written and conceived by Nehprii Ameni in response to the killings of Troy Davis, Trayvon Martin, and Kimani Gray, and as a reaction to the statistic that every 36 hours a young black person in the United States is killed extrajudicially by a police officer, security guard, or self-appointed law enforcer.
